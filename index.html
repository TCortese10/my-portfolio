About Me

Hi everyone! My name is Turner Cortese, I'm 26 years old, and I was born and raised in London, Ontario, Canada. Currently, I'm a first-year student enrolled in the two-year Computer Programming program at Humber College. When I'm not coding or studying, you'll find me analyzing and comparing sports statistics—especially NHL hockey, where I'm a devoted Toronto Maple Leafs fan...sadly. I also enjoy playing video games, watching movies, playing guitar, and hitting the ice for some weekly beer league. Outside of tech and sports, I love camping and spending time outdoors, often traveling with friends to explore new places. I currently work at a packaging company that prints and packs pharmaceutical products, but I'm eager to move up and take on new challenges in my career! At home, I have a pet cockatiel named Momo, and for a little fun fact—I can juggle! My life is a blend of technology, sports, music, and adventure, and I'm always looking to learn something new.
